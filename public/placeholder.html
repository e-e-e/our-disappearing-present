our disappearing present...

coming soon / 05:05:2016

There are no profiles, no permanence and no images in this artist-run social media network that will exists parasitically on across a number of websites. A prototype and a provisional statement towards what could be possible if social media had a different kind of politic, Our disappearing present channels Twitter and Snapchat, without the invasive commodification of your identity. Demonstrating a possible structure for our online world that sidesteps corporate control, this project is deliberately fleeting.

our disappearing present... will premiere as part of Next Wave 2016.
