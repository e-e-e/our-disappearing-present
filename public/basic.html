our disappearing present...

coming soon / 05:05:2016

There are no profiles, no permanence and no images in this artist-run social media network that will exist parasitically across a number of websites. A prototype and a provisional statement towards what could be possible if social media had a different kind of politic. Our disappearing present channels Twitter and Snapchat, without the invasive commodification of your identity. Demonstrating a possible structure for our online world that sidesteps corporate control, this project is deliberately fleeting.

our disappearing present... will premiere as part of Next Wave Festival 2016.

Our disappearing present was developed for Next Wave Festival 2016, and has been supported by The Margaret Lawrence Bequest.
