Our disappearing present...

There are no profiles, no permanence and no images in this artist-run social media network that exists parasitically across a number of websites. <Brainlina, Ships in the Night, Separating Hydrogen from Air>. A prototype and a provisional statement towards what could be possible if social media had a different kind of politic. Our disappearing present channels Twitter and Snapchat, without the invasive commodification of your identity. Demonstrating a possible structure for our online world that sidesteps corporate control, this project is deliberately fleeting.
